About this class

Bold. Delicious. And packed with flavour. Who doesn't love enchiladas? in this fast-paced class, geared toward the beginner, you'll learn the basics of making green chicken enchiladas with an array of traditional ingredients, using the time-honoured (read:secret) techniques of the Mexican kitchen, from charring on a comal to frying sauces, on your way to making luscious, deep-flavoured enchiladas.

About the tutor
Chef Majekadegbe Fishly
Lagos Nigeria

Olayemi Ibikunle has 2 years of experience in working in five private schools across Nigeria. She earned a B.Ed and a Masters in Teaching and Learning from Hazard University.
